What are the duties of the Local Authority?

   The Local Authority has broad duties to encourage, enable and assist
   young people, between the ages of 16-18, to participate in education,
   employment or training.

What are the duties of education providers?

   [366]The Education and Skills Act 2008 placed two Raising Participation
   Age related duties on education providers with regard to 16 and 17 year
   olds:
     * [367]Section 11 places a duty on education providers to promote
       good attendance to enable young people to meet their duty to
       participate.
     * [368]Section 13 places a duty on education providers to inform
       their local authority when a young person is no longer
       participating, for example, if they have dropped out.
